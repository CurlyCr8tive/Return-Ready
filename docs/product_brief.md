# Return Ready — Product Brief (Pivot)

## The Problem
Joanna is going on 12 weeks of parental leave with two unresolved problems:
1. Team data already exists in Google Sheets/Docs, but no synthesis exists.
2. She cannot stay current on AI news and trends while unreachable.

## The Solution
A private dashboard that:
- synthesizes weekly team data into biweekly and monthly reports
- generates weekly AI news and trends digests with Pursuit-specific implications
- requires no workflow changes from Joanna's team

## Feature A — Team Intelligence Reports

### Data Sources
- Team standups (Google Sheets)
- Weekly KPI docs (Google Docs)
  - Stef: attendance, pathfinder use, jobs, L3+
  - Greg: volunteers
  - Afiya: L1 cohort
  - Francis: admissions, community development

### Report Cadence
- Biweekly report every 2 weeks:
  - Biweekly Snapshot
  - KPI Highlights by Person
  - Patterns and Trends
  - Risks and Flags
  - Wins
- Monthly report at end of March, April, May:
  - The Month in Summary
  - KPI Performance by Person
  - What Improved
  - What Needs Attention
  - Heading Into Next Month

### Optional Management Tier
- Joanna connects her Google account in Settings
- selects management docs
- enables a separate management synthesis layer

## Feature B — AI News and Trends Digest

### Weekly Sections
- What Happened in AI This Week
- What It Means for Pursuit
- Who Is Hiring in AI
- Companies to Watch
- One Thing to Read

## Dashboard Views
1. Overview
2. Biweekly Reports
3. Monthly Reports
4. By Person
5. AI News Digest
6. Settings

## Runtime Flow

### Before Leave
Joanna shares team Sheets/Docs with service account once.

### Weekly
- pull connected docs
- generate AI digest

### Biweekly
- generate biweekly team report

### Monthly
- generate monthly report

### Return Day
Joanna reads all reports + by-person view + AI digests in under 2 hours.
